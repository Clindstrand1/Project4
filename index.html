New York Fashion Week is held twice a year, every year. This year, the Fall 2014 shows were presented February 6 through February 13, 2014. New York Fashion Week is sponsored each year by Mercedes Benz. Hundreds of designers participate and show at various locations throughout the city, which you can see on our maps app above. Check out the social conversation on twitter, instagram and youtube with the links above! Use the hashtag #NYFW to join in and share your opinions on your favorite designers and shows and what you're most excited to wear this fall.
